Botani Talk

Recipe (Tech Stack)

ESP32 + Soil Moisture Sensor + Real-time DB + AI API

Why I built it

My plants kept dying because I forgot to water them. I wanted a system that didn't just show boring data, but actually gave the plant an "emotional" voice to yell at me when it was thirsty.

Problems Faced

The cheap analog soil sensors kept corroding within a week due to electrolysis. Also, sending too many requests to the AI API was causing rate limits.

Trade-offs Made

Instead of leaving the sensor powered on 24/7 (which causes the corrosion), I coded the ESP32 to only send power to the sensor for 1 second every hour to take a reading. This saved the hardware but meant the data isn't down-to-the-second live.
